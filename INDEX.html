About Me

Multidisciplinary. Data-Informed. Community-First.

I'm a marketing and communications professional with nearly a decade of hands-on experience building digital strategies and communities. My expertise covers social media strategy, storytelling, SEO, content marketing, and community engagement, always data-informed, audience-driven, and results-oriented.

My portfolio spans multiple industries including finance, non-profit, entertainment, and post-secondary education across Canada and Nigeria. At the University of Lethbridge, I led alumni-focused digital engagement initiatives that grew audience reach by more than 70% in one year. Through my work with financial institutions and NGOs, I've helped deliver campaigns generating over 1 billion hashtag impressions and engagement rate lifts of up to 45%.

I was awarded a scholarship by the Government of Canada to pursue the prestigious CMA Chartered Marketer designation, a reflection of my commitment to strategic marketing leadership. Beyond strategy, I care deeply about building communities, including BUBO FPL and the Drive Thru newsletter, where sport enthusiasts, immigrants, and creators exchange ideas and grow together.

Common Questions

FAQs

What makes you different from an in-house hire?
I bring fresh eyes, tested strategies, and insights from working across multiple industries: finance, education, non-profit, telecom, and more. That cross-sector experience means I spot opportunities and patterns that someone working in a single vertical might miss. I also ramp up fast and focus purely on results from day one.
What kind of deliverables can I expect?
You can expect clear strategy documents, content plans, platform audits with actionable insights, and campaign execution support. I also provide performance reports and templates your team can use long after the project wraps. Everything built to drive results, not just sit in a folder.
How long will a project take and when will I see results?
Most projects take 8-10 weeks from start to finish. For awareness campaigns, you might see engagement within days. For SEO or lead generation, expect 12-15 weeks before meaningful data rolls in. I always set clear milestones and track progress from day one, so you're never guessing, just growing.
